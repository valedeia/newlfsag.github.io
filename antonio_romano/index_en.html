Antonio Romano

E-mail: antonio.romano(chiocciola)unito.it

Antonio Romano is Professor (habil. as Full Professor) at the Dept. of Foreign Languages and Literatures of the University of Turin and scientific director of the Laboratory of Experimental Phonetics "Arturo Genre" and Director of the Master in Translation for Cinema, TV and Multimedia.

After a graduation in 1994 at the Politecnico di Torino and a "Diplôme d'Études Approfondies" (D.E.A.) at the Université Stendhal in Grenoble (France), he attended a European PhD in Language Science in Grenoble. His doctoral dissertation (discussed in 1999) was judged "très honorable avec félicitations du Jury".

Address: c/o Dipartimento di Lingue e Letterature Straniere e Culture Moderne - Università di Torino - via Sant'Ottavio, 20 - 10124 Torino - Italy.

TEACHING APPOINTMENTS

His teaching activities in Italian Universities and abroad began in 1996. In the last years, he has been teaching in the following courses:

  • "General Linguistics" (54 hours) and "General Linguistics: Introduction to the study of the spoken languages" (27->54 hours) at the Dept. of Foreign Languages and Literatures of the University of Turin
  • "Glottology and Linguistics" at the School of Medicine of the University of Turin (Speech Therapy, 36 hours, and Audiometric Techniques, 24 hours).
  • "Glottology and Linguistics" at the Master in Traduzione per il Cinema, la TV e l'Editoria Multimediale (24h).
