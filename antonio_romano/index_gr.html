Antonio Romano is Researcher at the Faculty of Foreign Languages of the University of Turin and scientific director of the Laboratory of Experimental Phonetics "Arturo Genre".

After a graduation in 1994 at the Politecnico di Torino and a "Diplôme d'Études Approfondies" (D.E.A.) at the Université Stendhal in Grenoble (France), he attended a European PhD in Language Science in Grenoble. His doctoral dissertation (discussed in 1999) was judged "très honorable avec félicitations du Jury".

Addresss: c/o Dipartimento di Scienze del Linguaggio - Università di Torino - via Sant'Ottavio, 20 - 10124 Torino - Italia.
